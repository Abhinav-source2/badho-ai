# AI/ML Compensation Data 2024 — Levels.fyi Style

## United States — Total Compensation (Base + Bonus + Stock/year)

### Big Tech (Google, Meta, Apple, Amazon, Microsoft)
L3/E3 (Junior, 0-2 years):
- Base: $150,000 - $175,000
- Bonus: $20,000 - $30,000
- Stock/year: $40,000 - $80,000
- Total: $210,000 - $285,000

L4/E4 (Mid, 2-5 years):
- Base: $175,000 - $220,000
- Bonus: $30,000 - $50,000
- Stock/year: $80,000 - $180,000
- Total: $285,000 - $450,000

L5/E5 (Senior, 5-8 years):
- Base: $210,000 - $260,000
- Bonus: $50,000 - $80,000
- Stock/year: $150,000 - $350,000
- Total: $410,000 - $690,000

### AI-First Companies (OpenAI, Anthropic, Cohere, Mistral)
Premium of 20-40% over Big Tech for equivalent levels.
Heavy equity component — illiquid but potentially high value.
OpenAI L4 equivalent: $400,000 - $700,000 total comp reported.

### Series A-B Startups (US)
Lower cash, higher equity:
- Junior: $130,000 - $155,000 base + 0.2-0.5% equity
- Senior: $170,000 - $210,000 base + 0.1-0.3% equity
- Equity value at exit: highly variable, 0 to life-changing

## India — Total Compensation (LPA, all-in)

### Product Companies (Flipkart, Swiggy, CRED, Razorpay, Meesho)
L1/Junior (0-2 years): ₹18-30 LPA
L2/Mid (2-4 years): ₹30-55 LPA
L3/Senior (4-7 years): ₹55-100 LPA
L4/Staff (7+ years): ₹100-180 LPA

### Series A-B AI Startups (India)
20-30% equity premium vs product companies, lower cash:
Junior: ₹15-25 LPA + ESOPs
Mid: ₹25-45 LPA + ESOPs
Senior: ₹45-80 LPA + ESOPs
First AI hire (any experience): negotiable, often above-band + co-founder equity

### MNC India (Google, Meta, Microsoft India)
Google India ML/AI:
L3: ₹45-70 LPA
L4: ₹70-120 LPA
L5: ₹120-200 LPA
(Significantly below US comp, but top of India market)

## Negotiation Data Points
- Competing offers increase final comp by average 15-25%
- AI/ML premium over SWE at same company: 15-30%
- Remote roles: 10-15% discount to in-office at same company
- Fastest salary growth period: Year 2-4 (switching companies every 2 years
  averages 40-60% increase vs staying)

## What Affects Compensation Most (in order)
1. Company stage and funding (FAANG > AI-first > growth stage > Series A)
2. Years of experience (biggest single factor)
3. Competing offers (most actionable lever)
4. Location (SF > NYC > Seattle > remote)
5. Specialization (LLM infra > applied AI > general ML)
6. Interview performance (can move you 1-2 levels)
