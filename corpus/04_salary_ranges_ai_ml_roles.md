# AI/ML Engineer Salary Ranges 2024

## United States (USD/year)

### AI Engineer
- 0-2 years: $120,000 - $160,000
- 2-4 years: $160,000 - $210,000
- 4-7 years: $210,000 - $280,000
- 7+ years / Staff: $280,000 - $400,000+

### ML Engineer
- 0-2 years: $115,000 - $155,000
- 2-4 years: $155,000 - $200,000
- 4-7 years: $200,000 - $260,000
- 7+ years / Staff: $260,000 - $380,000+

### Data Scientist
- 0-2 years: $90,000 - $130,000
- 2-4 years: $130,000 - $175,000
- 4-7 years: $175,000 - $230,000
- 7+ years: $230,000 - $320,000+

### MLOps / AI Platform Engineer
- 0-2 years: $110,000 - $150,000
- 2-4 years: $150,000 - $195,000
- 4-7 years: $195,000 - $255,000
- 7+ years: $255,000 - $360,000+

## Location Multipliers (US)
- San Francisco Bay Area: 1.0x (base)
- New York City: 0.95x
- Seattle: 0.90x
- Austin: 0.80x
- Remote (US): 0.85-0.90x

## India (INR LPA)

### AI Engineer
- 0-2 years: ₹12-22 LPA
- 2-4 years: ₹22-40 LPA
- 4-7 years: ₹40-75 LPA
- 7+ years: ₹75-150 LPA+

### ML Engineer
- 0-2 years: ₹10-20 LPA
- 2-4 years: ₹20-38 LPA
- 4-7 years: ₹38-70 LPA
- 7+ years: ₹70-130 LPA+

### Data Scientist
- 0-2 years: ₹8-16 LPA
- 2-4 years: ₹16-30 LPA
- 4-7 years: ₹30-55 LPA
- 7+ years: ₹55-100 LPA+

## India City Multipliers
- Bangalore: 1.0x (base)
- Mumbai: 0.95x
- Hyderabad: 0.90x
- Pune: 0.85x
- Delhi/NCR: 0.90x
- Remote: 0.80-0.90x

## Equity at Startups (India)
- Series A: 0.1-0.5% typical for early engineers
- Series B: 0.05-0.2%
- Series C+: 0.01-0.1%
- ESOP vesting: typically 4 years with 1 year cliff

## Key Insight
AI/ML roles command a 30-50% premium over equivalent-experience backend roles
in both US and India markets as of 2024. This gap is expected to persist through
2026 due to talent shortage.
